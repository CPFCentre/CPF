① Post Details

The headline shown at the top of the post and in the blog list.
Your name, or just write "CPFC".
This becomes the filename, e.g. sportsday2024.html
1–2 sentences shown on the Blog index page as a preview.
The main photo shown at the top of the post. Use a photo from the Images folder.
Type the path to any image already in your project, or upload a new image to Images/uploads/ first and then enter its path here.

② Paste Your Content

Copy your text from Word or a text file and paste it below. Each blank line becomes a new paragraph automatically. Use the buttons to add subheadings or image references.

Tip: Leave a blank line between paragraphs. To reference a photo write [image: Images/yourphoto.jpg] on its own line — it will float right in the post.

③ Choose Sidebar Posts

Tick the existing posts you want to appear in the "Other Posts" sidebar on this new page.

New posts you create will automatically appear here the next time you open this importer after adding them to the KNOWN_POSTS list at the top of this file's script.
