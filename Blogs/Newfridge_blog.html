New Fridge, Oven, and Microwave

Author: Nabeel Chakera  •  Date: 17 January 2024

A heartwarming act of generosity recently transformed our kitchen facilities here at the Cerebral Palsy Foundation Centre. Thanks to the incredible kindness of local donors, we are proud to announce the installation of brand-new appliances: a state-of-the-art fridge, microwave, and oven.

The impact of this donation cannot be overstated. Our previous kitchen equipment was outdated and failing, making it challenging to prepare meals for those in need in our community. With the arrival of these modern appliances, our kitchen has been revitalised, and our capacity to serve nutritious meals has been greatly enhanced.

We extend our deepest gratitude to the anonymous donors whose generosity made this transformation possible. Your contribution not only improves the physical space of our kitchen but also uplifts the spirits of everyone who walks through our doors.

This upgrade marks a significant milestone in our mission to support and empower individuals and families in need. With your continued support, we look forward to making an even greater impact in the lives of those we serve.

Thank you once again for your unwavering commitment to our community.
