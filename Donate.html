Why Donate to CPFC?

Donating to the Cerebral Palsy Foundation Centre (CPFC) is an impactful way to support individuals with disabilities and promote inclusivity in communities. CPFC's comprehensive support services cater not only to those with cerebral palsy but also to individuals facing other disabilities such as blindness, deafness, and mutism.

By contributing to CPFC, donors play a vital role in ensuring that individuals with diverse needs receive the necessary resources, therapies, and assistance programmes to navigate life's challenges. Donations fuel community events and workshops, enabling CPFC to promote inclusivity and create environments where everyone feels valued and supported.

CPF's focus on research and innovation drives advancements in understanding and treating various disabilities. Donations fund research projects, technological innovations, and therapeutic interventions aimed at improving outcomes and enhancing the quality of life for individuals with disabilities.

Empowerment lies at the core of CPF's programmes, which aim to equip individuals with disabilities with the skills, training, and resources they need to live independently and achieve their goals.
